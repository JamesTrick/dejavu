Indicators are the backbone of any quantative trading strategy. They are mathematical calculations based on the price, volume, or open interest of a security. Indicators can help traders identify trends, momentum, volatility, and other market conditions.

Dejavu being a new library, currently only supports a handful of indicators and contributions are more than welcome.

Overtime, this list will grow, and I am to have a clean integration with [ta-lib](https://ta-lib.org/).
